What is bio Dye?

Biodyes are dyes from nature itself. Dyes created to eliminate the use of toxic dyes. Dyes which are 100% natural. 100% natural better than eco friendly dyes. Clean effluents with not only zero pollution to land and water but also waste can be used as manure aswell For more info visit www.biodye.in


Comfort
Comfortclose

Comfort is one major factor as Biodyed fabrics are skin friendly and are comfortable to wear, due to lack of irritating dyes and toxic mordants the fabric is breathable which regulates body temperature and gives a cool feel as compared to synthetic dyed fabrics.

Why should I buy from you and not from your competitor?
There are many brands who claim to provide natural dyed wear, but in reality they end up using semi-syenthetic dyes and toxic mordants obtain their colours. Biodye offers a wide range of natural colourants of exceptionally high quality, the result of nine years of rigorous research and development.due to the absence of toxic metals , synthetic dyes and harmful chemicals, it is 100% eco friendly. Biodye processes and materials adhere to recognized international organic standards and highest quality of dyeing is achieved. The dye house provides safe and fairtrade conditions for employment of rural people and the waste products from dyeing of the yarn/fabric are non-polluting :the effluent is clear and can be released onto farmland while the solid waste can be composed to yield manure.
