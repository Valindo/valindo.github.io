Meet plugzee


A tiny Little Device that turns any
audio speaker into a Bluetooth speaker without
compromising on its audio quality.


Pair your SmartPhone, Tablet or any other
Bluetooth-enabled device with Plugzee and
stream your music wirelessly.



Built-in Battery Upto 18 Hours



Charge via any Micro USB port



Works with all Bluetooth enabled devices

Plug and Play

Plugzee works with any audio speaker
with a 3.5mm audio jack and retrieves your audio
in the best quality possible.


So whether it's a party or an outing, turn any of your
speakers into a pair of Bluetooth speakers
by just plugging into a Plugzee

Tech Specs
